DC area COVID hospitalizations: experimental measures

The population-weighted version is the same as used by the original DC-area hospitalization tracker here. The employment-weighted version weights county hospitalization rates by employment shares, where the employment share of an county is that county’s share of total employment in DC coming from the eight counties, as reported by the Census OnTheMap tool for 2019.

Each county’s total employment in DC and associated share is the following:
